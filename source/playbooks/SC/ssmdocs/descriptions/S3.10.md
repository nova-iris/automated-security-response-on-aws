### Document Name - ASR-SC_2.3.0_S3.10

## What does this document do?
This document configures lifecycle policies on S3 buckets that have versioning enabled, to manage the transition and expiration of noncurrent object versions.

## Input Parameters
* Finding: (Required) Security Hub finding details JSON
* AutomationAssumeRole: (Required) The ARN of the role that allows Automation to perform the actions on your behalf.

## Output Parameters
* Remediation.Output
